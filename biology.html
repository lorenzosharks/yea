Amino acids properties!

  • Monomers of proteins
  • Comprised of 3 groups
  • H-N-H is the Amino group
  • O=C-OH is the Carboxyl group
  • C in the middle is the central carbon
  • The H above the central carbon is always there and attached to the central carbon
  • The R is not a atom but a thing called the R-Group
